Summary Overview

This theme focuses on algorithms that learn, adapt, and stabilize under feedback. The work spans actor-critic optimization, Nash equilibrium seeking, decentralized concurrent learning, cooperative gradient dynamics, and applied control for engineered systems.

The emphasis is on convergence, stability, deception-aware decision-making, momentum and restart mechanisms, and the practical behavior of learning algorithms when optimization is embedded in dynamic systems.
