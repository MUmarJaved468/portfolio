title: Stochastic Gauge-Hodge Theory Primer
status: draft
---

Stochastic Gauge-Hodge Theory studies random spaces equipped with gauge transport,
twisted Hodge structure, and admissible observables.

The guiding object is a twisted Hodge Laplacian that separates locally fluctuating
components from harmonic structure:

$$
\Delta_A = d_A^\ast d_A + d_A d_A^\ast
$$

The central question is what remains visible after observables are compressed onto
the harmonic sector, and when that visible component is enough to support rigidity
or reconstruction statements about the hidden geometric structure.
